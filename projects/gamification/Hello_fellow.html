Hello Fellows


Piramal Foundation
Type: Gamification & Curriculum Design
Target group: 20-25yrs
Work: Social play, Organisation Behaviour, Mental Health
Role: Play Designer, Research


Hello Fellows is a gamified curriculum  design cratering to youth undergoing a Fellowship programme called Gandhi Fellowship through developing a playful organisation culture.
The objective of the fellowship is to Bring together youth who aspire to make an impact on the world around them Offer the youth opportunity to experience first-hand what it takes to make an impact Build the leadership ability of youth by facilitating in them the 6 attributes that nation-builder must-have. -self Awareness, collaborative work, influence without authority, Excellence, articulating values and identifying own private dreams.
The project outcome is in the form of games, activities, toolkit and playful rituals embedded in fellowship processes.
These processes consist of :
Development of leadership components. Management of fellow’s mental and physical wellness
Quirky communication devices
Tools and resources to help fellow in the management of their schedule
Workplace and Space-design for wellness
Tracking maps and planners to inculcate self-awareness,assessment of behavioural patterns through self-refection
The role-playing platform for: individual fellow's concerns
Incorporating social play using interactive exercise and games
